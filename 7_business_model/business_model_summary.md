# 💸 Business & Revenue Model Summary - Solo Accounting

This directory outlines the financial strategy, market dynamics, and operational sustainability models for **Solo Accounting**. Our core commercial objective is **sustainable utility**—delivering a secure, privacy-respecting cloud accounting SaaS tool at a price point that covers costs, satisfies state compliance, and provides a sustainable operating surplus.

## 📈 Strategic Financial Principles

Our business strategy is defined by three fundamental principles:

1. **Serverless Infrastructure Arbitrage:** 
   By leveraging edge computing (Cloudflare Workers) and on-demand PostgreSQL serverless databases, we keep dynamic compute costs extremely low. This lightweight serverless architecture provides a cost baseline that legacy, centralized SaaS competitors cannot match.
   
2. **Breakeven Integrity:**
   We are not chasing VC-backed hockey-stick growth. Our prices are kept exceptionally low ($7/mo or $70/yr) to cover standard automated banking connection integrations (Teller/Plaid) and secure, cached AI token consumption. 
   
3. **High Margin of Safety:**
   Our extremely lean operating costs ($250/mo fixed overhead) mean the project is virtually immune to financial stress, achieving cash neutrality with less than 65 customers.
