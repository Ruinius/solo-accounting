# 🚲 Solopreneur & Contractor Add-ons

Unlike large corporations with dedicated accounting departments, micro-business owners and freelancers often manage complex personal and business lives that blur together. **Solo Accounting** features specialized, lightweight tools tailored for solopreneurs to track mileage, write off business expenses safely, and separate personal finances without needing multiple complex tools.

---

## 💡 What are Solopreneur Add-ons? (In Plain English)

As a solopreneur, you are your business. 
* You might occasionally buy business supplies using a personal credit card, or pay for a personal dinner using your business checking account by mistake (called **co-mingling**).
* You probably use your personal car to visit clients or make deliveries. Under the law, every mile you drive for business can save you over 60 cents on your taxes, but tracking those miles manually in a paper notebook is incredibly tedious.

Solo Accounting handles these hybrid scenarios gracefully with simple, specialized tools.

---

## 🚗 Smart Mileage & Vehicle Expense Tracking

For independent contractors and gig economy drivers (like our persona **Diana Gigworker**), vehicle expenses are their single largest tax write-off. Solo Accounting includes an easy-to-use mileage tracker:

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              🚗 Business Mileage Log                  │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ • Date: May 27, 2026                                   │
│ • Purpose: Catering delivery to Central Plaza          │
│ • Distance: 24.5 Miles                                 │
│ • Tax Deduction Saved: $16.42                          │
│                                                        │
│ [ Match to Gas Receipt ]       [ Add to Tax Log ]      │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

* **Standard Mileage Method:** Quickly log miles with a purpose. Solo automatically computes the dollar value of the deduction using the latest official government standard rate.
* **Actual Expenses Method:** If you choose to deduct actual gas, repairs, and insurance rather than standard mileage, Solo helps you allocate a "Business Use Percentage" (e.g. 70% business, 30% personal) to your vehicle expenses, automatically splitting them in your ledger.
* **Depreciation Helper:** A simple, layman-friendly wizard that calculates your vehicle's yearly depreciation value so you aren't surprised by the true cost of vehicle wear-and-tear.

## 🧹 Co-Mingling Cleanup & Pocket-Money Helper

To fix accidental cross-payments between personal and business accounts:

> [!IMPORTANT]
> When you buy business paper on a personal card, Solo Accounting registers this as **"Owner's Contribution"** (increasing your business equity) rather than messy debt.
> When you accidentally buy a personal coffee on your business card, simply swipe the transaction to mark it as **"Draw / Personal Expense"**. It is instantly removed from your business Profit & Loss statement and recorded as a personal withdrawal, keeping your business tax books perfectly clean.

This simple swiping interface makes daily bookkeeping feel like checking a social media feed, taking less than 5 minutes a week.
