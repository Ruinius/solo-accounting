# 📸 Document & Receipt Processing

Receipt collection is one of the biggest headaches for small business owners. **Solo Accounting** completely simplifies this by allowing you to instantly upload receipts, automatically extract receipt details, and attach them to your ledger transactions—all without needing a bulky, dedicated App Store app.

---

## 💡 How Document Processing Works (In Plain English)

You buy baking supplies or trade tools, and the cashier hands you a crumpled receipt. In the past, you'd store these in a shoebox, only to lose half of them by tax season.

With Solo Accounting:
1. **Snap or Send:** You open the Solo web app on your phone's browser, take a quick photo of the receipt, and upload it. Alternatively, you can forward PDF invoices from your email inbox directly to your account.
2. **AI Optical Reader (OCR):** Our secure AI scans the receipt image. It instantly pulls out the date, the vendor's name (e.g. *Home Depot*), the tax paid, and the total amount.
3. **Auto-Match:** The system searches your bank feed for a matching $45.82 charge on that exact date and automatically pins the receipt to the transaction. 

---

## 📱 Mobile-Friendly Uploads (No Native App Store Download Needed)

Instead of forcing you to download a heavy native iOS or Android app that drains your battery and demands updates, Solo Accounting is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. 

> [!TIP]
> You simply go to your Solo Accounting website on your iPhone or Android browser, click "Add to Home Screen", and it behaves exactly like a native app. When you tap "Upload Receipt", it launches your phone's native camera immediately so you can snap and upload in seconds.

## 📂 CSV & PDF Statement Processing

If you don't connect your bank feed automatically, Solo Accounting offers a simple CSV importer:
* **Drag-and-Drop Statement Importer:** Drag your monthly bank statement (PDF or CSV) directly into the window.
* **Header Matching Helper:** The software visualizes your table columns and lets you map "Date", "Description", and "Amount" in seconds.
* **Tax Archive Safeguard:** All your uploaded receipts and statements are organized in a standard directory structure (e.g. `/receipts/2026/05/`) and can be exported as a single ZIP file for your CPA. This gives you a bulletproof defense against audits.
